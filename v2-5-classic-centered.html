My Approach

Hi, I'm Vivian.

I believe that no two bodies are the same, which means no two massage sessions should be either. My practice is rooted in providing an intuitive, personalized bodywork experience designed to give your body exactly what it needs in the moment.

Rather than following a rigid routine, I listen to your muscles and adapt my technique in real-time. Each custom session blends the best of multiple modalities to achieve both deep relief and profound relaxation.
